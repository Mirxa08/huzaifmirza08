Lahore Traffic Hotspots

This Grafana dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of traffic hotspots in Lahore, Pakistan. It combines geospatial mapping, traffic level visualization, and data analytics to deliver insights that can support city planning and transportation management.

Key Features

- Sunburst diagram highlighting the most congested locations
- Traffic hotspots map with color-coded intensity levels
- Count base chart categorizing hotspots by traffic severity
- Bubble and pie charts offering supplementary insights

Use Cases and Benefits

The dashboard empowers stakeholders, such as city planners and transportation authorities, to make data-driven decisions to improve traffic management and infrastructure in Lahore. The insights can help identify bottlenecks, optimize traffic flow, and guide targeted interventions to address mobility challenges
